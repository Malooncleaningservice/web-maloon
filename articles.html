The Benefits of Green Cleaning for Your Business

June 1, 2026 5 min read

In today's environmentally conscious world, more businesses are turning to green cleaning solutions. At Maloon Cleaning Service, we specialize in eco-friendly cleaning that benefits both your business and the environment.

Improved Indoor Air Quality

Traditional cleaning products often contain harsh chemicals that can linger in the air, affecting employee health and productivity. Our green cleaning products are free from volatile organic compounds (VOCs) and other harmful substances.

Enhanced Employee Productivity

Studies show that employees working in clean, healthy environments are more productive and take fewer sick days. Green cleaning creates a workspace that supports employee well-being.

Environmental Responsibility

By choosing green cleaning, your business reduces its environmental footprint. Our LEED-certified programs help you meet sustainability goals while maintaining high cleanliness standards.

Post-Construction Cleaning Checklist

May 15, 2026 7 min read

Construction projects leave behind dust, debris, and mess that require professional cleanup. Our comprehensive post-construction cleaning checklist ensures your site is ready for occupancy.

Phase 1: Rough Cleaning

  • Remove large debris and construction waste
  • Sweep and vacuum all surfaces
  • Clean windows and glass surfaces
  • Dust removal from walls, ceilings, and fixtures

Phase 2: Detailed Cleaning

  • Floor cleaning and polishing
  • Sanitization of restrooms and kitchens
  • Final dusting of all surfaces
  • Exterior pressure washing

How Often Should You Clean Your Office?

April 28, 2026 4 min read

The frequency of office cleaning depends on several factors including business type, size, foot traffic, and industry requirements. Here's our guide to finding the right schedule.

Daily Cleaning

High-traffic areas like restrooms, break rooms, and reception areas benefit from daily cleaning to maintain hygiene and professional appearance.

Weekly Cleaning

Most offices require weekly cleaning for dusting, vacuuming, and general maintenance. This frequency balances cleanliness with budget considerations.

Monthly Deep Cleaning

Monthly deep cleaning addresses areas that don't need frequent attention but still require regular maintenance for long-term cleanliness.
