Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, including:

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under age 18 without verification of parental consent, we will take steps to remove that information from our servers.
