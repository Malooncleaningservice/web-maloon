## Best Practices Applied

✅ **F-Pattern Reading** - Important info on left, supporting on right
✅ **Chunking** - Information grouped into digestible pieces
✅ **Visual Hierarchy** - Size, color, and position indicate importance
✅ **Progressive Disclosure** - Basic info first, details below
✅ **Proximity** - Related items grouped together
✅ **Contrast** - Important elements stand out
✅ **White Space** - Generous spacing prevents overwhelm
